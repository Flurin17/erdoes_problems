# Erdős Problem #421

Source: <https://www.erdosproblems.com/421>  
Fetched from the site's LaTeX-source view on 2026-07-16.

## Verbatim statement

Is there a sequence $1\leq d_1<d_2<\cdots$ with density $1$ such that all products
\[
\prod_{u\leq i\leq v}d_i
\]
are distinct?

## Source ambiguity

The page does not define “density.” The solving run must distinguish natural density from lower density and must state precisely which version its construction proves.

## Initial goal

Independently construct such a sequence and prove both density one and injectivity of the interval-product map.
