# Erdős Problem #469

Source: <https://www.erdosproblems.com/469>  
Fetched from the site's LaTeX-source view on 2026-07-16.

## Verbatim statement

Let $A$ be the set of all $n$ such that $n=d_1+\cdots+d_k$ with $d_i$ distinct proper divisors of $n$, but this is not true for any $m\mid n$ with $m<n$. Does
\[
\sum_{n\in A}\frac1n
\]
converge?

## Initial goal

Derive enough structural restrictions on the minimal numbers in $A$ to prove reciprocal-sum convergence, or construct a divergent subfamily.
