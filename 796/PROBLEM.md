# Erdős Problem #796

Source: <https://www.erdosproblems.com/796>  
Fetched from the site's LaTeX-source view on 2026-07-16.

## Verbatim statement

Let $k\geq2$ and let $g_k(n)$ be the largest possible size of $A\subseteq\{1,\ldots,n\}$ such that every $m$ has $<k$ solutions to $m=a_1a_2$ with $a_1<a_2\in A$.

Is it true that
\[
g_3(n)=\frac{\log\log n}{\log n}n+(c+o(1))\frac{n}{\log n}
\]
for some constant $c$?

## Initial goal

Construct and bound near-extremal sets sharply enough to identify the second-order constant $c$.
