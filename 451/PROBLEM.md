# Erdős Problem #451

Source: <https://www.erdosproblems.com/451>  
Fetched from the site's LaTeX-source view on 2026-07-16.

## Verbatim statement

Estimate $n_k$, the smallest integer $>2k$ such that
\[
\prod_{1\leq i\leq k}(n_k-i)
\]
has no prime factor in $(k,2k)$.

## Initial goal

Build explicit admissible intervals $[n_k-k,n_k-1]$ and derive lower bounds on their first possible right endpoint, seeking matching asymptotic growth for $n_k$.
